What is Microsoft Ignite After Party?

Microsoft Ignite After Party is where our local community members come together to watch a pre-selected event, discuss about it, bringing content closer to the community and going deep-dive into a particular topic. Be a part of the After Party and experience authenticity, diversity and inclusivity that we bring as a part of Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador.
At the Microsoft Ignite 2022 AfterParty hosted by MLSA India, we watched "Focus Into AI" breakout session. Also, meet community leaders (our spotlight guests). Along with this, we did a Q&A session with our Q&A speaker guest from Microsoft. Finally, there was a quiz.
